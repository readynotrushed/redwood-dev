Why this exists

Most children aren't joining.

Informal polls across four Redwood classes showed that roughly 70% of parents said they hadn't been joining the Zoom sessions. Not because they didn't want to, but because of three specific reasons: Timing didn't work. Toddlers have unpredictable schedules. Access friction.

🌍

Time zones

A significant number of Redwood families are temporarily abroad, many in the UK and Europe. A 10am Dubai session is 7 or 8am for them. They're not disengaged. The time just doesn't work.

🌙

Toddler schedules

You can't tell a 2 year old that Zoom starts in five minutes. Some children are still sleeping. Others are in the middle of focussed play. Fixed session times and toddler schedules don't always align.

🔗

Finding the link

Parents were asking in WhatsApp groups at 9:36am: "Is the call on?" and "Has anyone got the link?" The session was already starting. No matter how simple it is, people are stressed, tired and even the tiniest friction can be insurmountable.

💡

This system doesn't replace your existing sessions. Teachers can keep their regular class calls can. Tthe current system works for children who show up. This is for the ones who don't, and the reasons they don't are fixable..

What is this?

A drop in schedule for remote learning days

Think of it as the Redwood common area, online. You offer a 15 minute session whenever it works for you. Parents check the live schedule and join what fits their day.

1

Pick any 15 minute slot

Morning, afternoon, or a later slot for families who are abroad. According to feedback, parents would like to join the sessions but sometimes the times don't work for them or 30 minutes is too much of a commitment.

2

You host whatever feels natural

A song, a story, some phonics, a dance break. Keep it light. 15 minutes of genuine engagement with a familiar face is what children need right now.

3

Any Redwood child can join, not just your class

Siblings can attend together. Children whose teacher isn't available can still see a familiar Redwood face. Parents choose the sessions that fits their day. The schedule is open to the whole school.

4

No coordination needed

You don't need to check with anyone before booking. If two teachers pick the same slot, that's fine - parents just have more options to choose from. The schedule updates in real time.

💡

On names: Ask parents to rename their Zoom display to their child's name before joining. When children join from outside your usual class, hearing their name called out will increase their sense of belonging.

Questions

Questions you might have

If something isn't answered here, use the WhatsApp button below.

Do I need to book in advance? +

No. You can book five minutes before your session. The schedule updates in real time. Of course the sooner, the better.

What if I book the same time as another teacher? +

That's completely fine. Both sessions appear on the parent schedule and families choose whichever works for them. More options is better for parents.

Can children outside my class join? +

Yes, and that's the point. Any Redwood child can attend any session. You don't need to do anything differently, just treat everyone who joins as a Redwood child, because they are.

What if I don't know a child's name? +

Parents are asked to rename their Zoom display to their child's name before joining. If someone hasn't done that, just ask them or ask the child "what's your name?". It's a perfectly fine way to start.

How long should a session be? +

15 minutes and that's intentional. It matches toddler attention spans and keeps the commitment manageable for you and for the parents. If the energy is good and you want to run a little over, that's your call. If you had already planned classes of 30 minutes, just add the same zoom link twice: once for the first 15 minutes, and once for the last 15 minutes.

Can I book multiple slots in one day? +

Yes. Book as many as you like. One is already a contribution. Two or three gives parents more options throughout the day.

I'm abroad. Can I still participate? +

Absolutely. The schedule has a timezone selector. Afternoon slots in Dubai (marked 🌍) may be perfect morning slots wherever you are. Families abroad are in the same situation and have been asking for later sessions.

💬

Something not working?

There's a "Need support?" button on the booking page that goes straight to Luna, who can help you with questions or solving problems with the system. Feedback always welcome.
